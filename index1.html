About the Project

This project was created as part of our Design Thinking and Innovation coursework, aimed at making book discovery engaging and intuitive.

Problem Statement

Many readers struggle to find books they love across genres without a central, visual, and interactive platform.

User Research

Through surveys and interviews, we learned readers appreciate visual previews, quick access to genres, and mobile-friendly design.

Ideation & Prototyping

We brainstormed various layouts and selected a responsive, animated interface with genre-based filtering and book previews.
